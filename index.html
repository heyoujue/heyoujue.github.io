    Hi! Welcome to my homepage. My name is Youjue He; or 何He[xɤ˧˥]友You[jəu˨˩˦]珏Jue[ʨyə˧˥] in Chinese order. I come from Whenzhou, Zhejiang, China, and the local dialect is my first language. So the homepage is named after my name articulated in my mother tongue, 何Vu[vɯ˧˩]友Yau[jaɯ˦˥]珏Ko[ko˧˨˧]. Currently, I am a Ph.D. student at Department of Chinese Language and Literature, Peking University.
